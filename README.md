## Key Features of Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP Implementation

### Financial Management
- **Oracle Financials Cloud**: General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Cash Management, Fixed Assets, Tax Management
- **Multi-GAAP support**: US GAAP, IFRS, Mexican NIIF
- **Continuous close capabilities**: Period-end close reduced from 15 days to 5 days
- **Embedded SOX controls**: Continuous monitoring for regulatory compliance

### Supply Chain Management
- **Oracle SCM Cloud**: Inventory Management, Order Management, Logistics, Product Lifecycle Management
- **Real-time inventory visibility**: Across all channels (stores, e-commerce, distribution centers)
- **AI-powered demand forecasting**: 20% reduction in stockouts, 15% reduction in excess inventory
- **Intelligent order orchestration**: BOPIS, ship-to-home, jobsite delivery

### Human Capital Management
- **Oracle HCM Cloud**: Core HR, Payroll, Talent Management, Workforce Management, Learning
- **Mobile-first HR experiences**: For 503,000 associates across North America
- **Integrated learning management**: Product knowledge certifications
- **Self-service capabilities**: Employee and manager self-service

### Procurement
- **Oracle Procurement Cloud**: Supplier Management, Purchasing, Sourcing, Contract Management
- **AI-powered spend analytics**: $50M+ in annual savings opportunities
- **Supplier risk monitoring**: Performance scorecards and risk assessment
- **Self-service procurement**: Store supplies and equipment

### Enterprise Performance Management
- **Oracle EPM Cloud**: Financial Planning, Budgeting, Consolidation, Account Reconciliation
- **Scenario modeling**: Multiple planning approaches
- **Real-time analytics**: Business intelligence and reporting

### Risk Management
- **Oracle Risk Management Cloud**: Financial Controls, Access Certification, Continuous Monitoring
- **SoD controls**: Segregation of duties enforcement
- **Continuous monitoring**: Real-time risk detection and mitigation

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1 (FY2026): Foundation
- Oracle Financials Cloud deployment across all entities
- Oracle Procurement Cloud for indirect spend
- Oracle HCM Cloud Phase 1 (Core HR, Payroll)
- Integration with existing POS (NCR) and e-commerce (Salesforce) systems

### Phase 2 (FY2027): Expansion
- Oracle SCM Cloud for inventory and order management
- Oracle EPM Cloud for financial planning and consolidation
- Oracle HCM Cloud Phase 2 (Talent, Learning, Workforce Management)
- Oracle Risk Management Cloud deployment

### Phase 3 (FY2028): Optimization
- Oracle CX Cloud integration for customer 360 view
- Advanced analytics and AI capabilities across all modules
- Full retirement of legacy systems
- Continuous improvement and optimization
