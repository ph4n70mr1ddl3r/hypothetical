### 7.2 Change Control Process

1. **Change Request**: Submit change request with business justification
2. **Impact Assessment**: Assess impact on other documents and processes
3. **Review**: Document owner reviews and approves/rejects
4. **Implementation**: Make changes following document standards
5. **Communication**: Notify stakeholders of changes
6. **Version Control**: Update version and revision history

### 7.3 Version Control Standards

- **Major Version** (X.0): Significant content changes, restructure
- **Minor Version** (X.Y): Minor updates, clarifications, new sections
- **Patch Version** (X.Y.Z): Typographical errors, formatting, references
